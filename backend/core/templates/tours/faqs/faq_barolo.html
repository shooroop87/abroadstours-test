Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer other wine tasting tours?

Yes! From Milan, we can privately arrange the Oltrepò Pavese wine tasting tour—famous for Pinot Noir—and the Amarone wine tasting tour combined with Verona or Lake Garda.

For group tours, Barolo is the best option from Milan: no traffic, a peaceful, non-touristy area, and truly amazing food and landscapes.

I'm new to wine. Will I enjoy the tour?

Absolutely! This tour is perfect for beginners. You'll learn the secrets behind one of Italy's best red wines—Barolo—plus enjoy other delicious wines like fruity Barbera and refreshing white wines ideal for summer or aperitivo.

Can I book a private tour?

Of course! We specialize in small private tours tailored just for you. Whether it's wine tastings at multiple wineries, a gourmet lunch at a top restaurant, or even truffle hunting, we'll customize everything to your wishes.

Do you offer hotel pickup?

For private tours, hotel pickup and drop-off are included. For group tours, our meeting point is easy to reach by subway—just a 10-minute ride from Duomo Square—or by taxi/Uber.

Is the tour wheelchair or stroller accessible?

Due to some staircases and uneven terrain, this tour is not recommended for wheelchairs or strollers.

Can I cancel for free?

Yes! Cancel free of charge up to 24 hours before your tour.

Plus, here's something unique: if you don't show up, we still refund 25%—no questions asked.

What if it rains?

Rain is rare in Alba and the Piedmont region, and Nebbiolo grapes need a dry climate.

A few raindrops only make the vineyard hills more magical. Just bring comfortable shoes and suitable clothing.

Do I pay less when booking directly on your website?

Booking directly with us means you always pay the lowest available price—guaranteed.

If you find our tour listed for less elsewhere, simply show us and we'll refund you double the difference (valid only for direct bookings on our website).

And beyond the savings, you're supporting a passionate local business—not a big corporation.

Yes! Reserve your spot now and pay nothing until 48 hours before the tour starts. Just select "zero deposit" when booking.
